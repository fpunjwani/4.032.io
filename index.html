Fahad is a grad student in MIT Integrated Design and Management program. He comes to Cambridge from Karachi, Pakistan, by way of Houston. Texas. 1. Twelve Clocks

Goal: Design and code twelve clocks
Design Principle: Why, when and how do people use clocks? A clock serves to tell and measure time.
Tell: What is the time right now? What is happening right now? What should I be doing in this time right now?
Measure: How much time has passed? What have I done in the time passed?

4. Sense of Community

Goal: Share my qualitative research with community builders so they can build better, stronger communities. My audience is urban, liberal, American community builders. Think: City of Boston's Mayor's Office or Indivisible, a national political organization. These folks value community building. They are experts in their jobs and are looking for experienced voices to help shape their programming. They are action-oriented. They want to collaborate instead of being instructed to adopt a certain way of working.
Design Principles:

  • How can I build trust and credibility with the viewer?
  • How can I empower viewers to take action?
  • How I can invite viewers to be co-creators?
